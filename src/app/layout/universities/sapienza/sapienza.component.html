Admissions

Step 1. Find the course of your interest here.

Learn more about your course. Different courses require different documents and academic background. Especially, in Masters programs there might be course-specific requirements. Make sure your academic background suits the program you want to apply for.


Step 2. Do the online pre-selection registration.

You can apply to two programs of your level (Bachelor's or Master's). General deadline for pre-selection process is from November 2020 - May/July 2021.


Step 3. Do the pre-enrollment registration.

Once you are admitted and you confirm you pre-acceptance, the letter will be issued as confirmation. You will use that letter to complete the pre-enrollment process.

NOTE: pre-selection and pre-enrollment are two distinct processes. Check the pre-enrollment website for deadlines.

Step 4. Visa interview.


Step 5. Finalize your enrollment at Sapienza.

After you arrive to Italy, you will have to pay the registration fee and finalize your enrollment until November 11, 2021.


N.B: There is no application fee in Sapienza University of Rome.

Eligibility

  • To be eligible for Italian higher education at the Bachelors level, it is mandatory to have a High School diploma earned after no less than 12 years of studies. If you do not possess this requirement, please visit Foundation Year webpage.

  • To be eligible for Italian higher education at the Masters level, it is mandatory to have a High School diploma and a Bachelors degree earned after no less than 15 years of studies (of which at least 3 must be at university level).



Required documents

Exact list of required documents can be found while doing the pre-selection registration. However, general requirements are as follows:

  • Valid Passport or ID document

  • Proof of studies (12 years for Bachelor's degree and at least 3 years of university for Master's degree)

  • Transcript of records

  • Language proficiency proof (at least B2: IELTS, CEFR, TOEFL, etc). Requirements vary according to the degree level and program

  • CV

  • Letter(s) of recommendation (Master's)



Scholarships

Just like some other Italian regions, the region of Lazio also supports talented and promising students financially, by the local financial assistance program called "LAZIODISCO". In order to be able to apply for regional scholarship a student is required to be admitted one of the Bachelor's or Master's programs mentioned above!



LAZIODISCO - How to apply

Step 1. From June 3 to July 24, 2021, complete online application at www.laziodisco.it.

To complete your application, you will mainly need to upload 4 documents. All these documents must be translated into Italian and confirmed by an official authorities.
  • Valid passport
  • Family income for year 2019 (January-December)
  • Family composition (the list of family members residing together given by official authorities)
  • Assets declaration (House is enough)

Step 2. After coming to Italy, bring the originals of above documents to designated CAF office to obtain your ISEE Universitario.

Step 3. Submit the original documents and the ISEE Universitario to the LAZIODISCO office.
